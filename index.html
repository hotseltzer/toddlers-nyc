Keeping Toddlers Busy

And Wallets Full

Stay-at home moms and dads, as well as nannies are always looking for something to do with their toddlers. I try to take my 2 year-old daughter out often so that she can use up some of that exhausting toddler energy.

I've created a list of places in Manhattan, mostly downtown because that is where we live, of fun, free/inexpensive places to let your kid run wild. If you are adventurous enough to brave subways and stairs with strollers, diapers and multiple toddlers, I've included some places in the other boroughs worth the trek.
