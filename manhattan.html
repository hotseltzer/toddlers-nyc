Tony Dapolito Outdoor Pool

This pool is a little gem. It is a free community pool without any lines. They even provide a free kids lunch.

Minetta Playground

There are many playgrounds in New York but if you are finding yourself lookng for a shady playground, this is the one. There are swings, play structures, and a water fountain for summer play

Chelsea Piers Toddler Gym

On really hot days or raining cold days, this place is refuge. Admission is $12 or $10 when you buy a 5 pack. The space is small enough that you don't have to run after your child. There are climbing structires and a ball pit

New York Kids Club

NY Kids Club requires class registration to particpate in their open play; however, they have pajama parties Friday and Saturday nights where you can drop off your 2.5 year old + child for 2 hours. They get to have fun and you get to have dinner without them
