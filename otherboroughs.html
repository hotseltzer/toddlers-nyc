Bronx Zoo

This huge zoo will keep your kids busy. But only go on Wednesday when admission is suggested donation

New York Botanical Garden

Although you have to pay admission, there is the wonderful Everett Children's Adventure Garden

Prospect Park Zoo

This zoo is small but really enjoyable to walk around. You won't leave feeling exhausted. The best part is that it costs $8 for admission

New York Kids Club

NY Kids Club requires class registration to particpate in their open play; however, they have pajama parties Friday and Saturday nights where you can drop off your 2.5 year old + child for 2 hours. They get to have fun and you get to have dinner without them
